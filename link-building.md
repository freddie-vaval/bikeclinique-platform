## Link Building Tactics

### 1. Guest Posting (Priority)

**Target Sites:**
- Bike industry blogs
- Cycling news sites
- Business/SaaS blogs
- Local business directories

**Sample Outreach:**
```
Subject: Guest Post for [Site] - Bike Shop Software

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out from BikeClinique - we're a SaaS platform helping bike shops manage bookings, inventory, and customers.

I'd love to contribute a guest post on [relevant topic]. 

Topics I can cover:
- How bike shops can 2x bookings with online booking
- Best software for bike shops in 2026
- How to start a bike repair business

Would you be interested? Happy to write something exclusive for your audience.

Best,
[Name]
```

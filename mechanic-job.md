# BikeClinique - Senior Mechanic Recruitment

## Job Title
**Senior Bike Mechanic** - Full-time

## Location
BikeClinique, [Your Location]

## Salary
£28,000 - £38,000 depending on experience + benefits

## About BikeClinique
We're a premium bike shop SaaS platform serving cycling businesses across the UK. We're growing our workshop services and need an experienced mechanic to join our team.

## The Role
We're looking for a skilled bike mechanic with advanced experience in:

### Essential Skills:
- **Advanced Groupsets:** SRAM, Shimano, Campagnolo (electronic & mechanical)
- **Servicing:** Full bike builds, tune-ups, emergency repairs
- **Custom Builds:** Bespoke bike builds, wheel building, fitting
- **Diagnostics:** Troubleshooting drivetrain, braking, suspension issues

### What You'll Do:
- Perform advanced bike servicing and repairs
- Build custom bikes from components
- Consult with customers on technical requirements
- Maintain workshop quality standards
- Mentor junior staff

### Requirements:
- 3+ years professional bike mechanic experience
- City & Guilds or equivalent qualification (preferred)
- Strong knowledge of modern groupsets (12-speed, wireless electronic)
- Excellent attention to detail
- Good customer communication skills

### Nice to Have:
- Wheel building certification
- Suspension fork service experience
- Experience with premium brands (Pinarello, Cervelo, Trek, etc.)

## Benefits:
- Competitive salary
- Training & development
- Staff bike discount
- Modern workshop
- Career progression

# BikeClinique Competitor Research Update - Feb 24

## Quick Overview
BikeClinique is positioning as a "best of both worlds" solution combining Bikebook's modern UI with Hubtiger's robust features, plus Shopify/Stripe integration.

---

## Market Landscape

### Current Players
1. **Bikebook** - Modern UI, customer portal focus, weaker backend
2. **Hubtiger** - Feature-rich, dated UI, strong job management
3. **Workshop Hero** - UK-focused, mid-market
4. **RotaKing** - Scheduling focus, less bike-specific

### Gap Analysis
| Feature | Bikebook | Hubtiger | Gap |
|---------|----------|----------|-----|
| Modern UI | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ BikeClinique |
| Job cards | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ BikeClinique |
| Collection/Delivery | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ BikeClinique |
| Shopify integration | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ BikeClinique |
| Embedded booking widget | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ BikeClinique |

---

## Opportunity Areas

### 1. UK Market
- Fewer players than US
- More receptive to modern SaaS
- Can start local, scale globally

### 2. Shopify Integration (Major Gap)
- Bike shops using Shopify for retail
- No current competitor connects bike-specific job management with e-commerce
- Potential for inventory sync, parts sales, service payments

### 3. AI Features (Future)
- Automated customer communication
- Predictive maintenance reminders
- Smart pricing recommendations
- AI-generated service summaries

## Differentiation Strategy

**Position:** "Modern bike shop management that actually works"

**Target:** 
- Independent bike shops (50-500 bikes/month)
- Multi-location bike businesses
- Mobile bike repair services

**Pricing:** £49-199/month (Hubtiger is £99+, Bikebook is similar)

**Go-to-market:**
1. Bike shop forums/communities
2. UK cycling events
3. Partner with bike shop suppliers
4. Content marketing (bike shop owners subreddit, cycling podcasts)

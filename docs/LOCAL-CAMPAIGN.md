# BikeClinique Local Marketing Campaign
## "Make Locals Aware" - Free & Low-Cost Assets

---

## 🎯 Objective
Get locals in London to know BikeClinique exists for:
1. Bike repairs & servicing
2. Selling premium used bikes (gravel + e-bikes)
3. AI Content services (future)

---

## 🚀 Quick Wins (This Week)

### 1. Google Business Profile (IF NOT DONE)
- ✅ Claim/optimize Google Business Profile
- ✅ Add photos, services, hours
- ✅ Get first 5 reviews (ask mates, family)
- 📍 Location: London (add exact address)

### 2. WhatsApp Group
- Create "BikeClinique Community" WhatsApp group
- Add existing customers, mates, local cycling clubs
- Post: tips, deals, before/after bikes
- **Free**: Just phone contacts

### 3. Local Facebook Groups
Post in these London groups (find 10+):
- "Cycling in London"
- "[YOUR AREDA] Residents"
- "London Bike Shops"
- "Southwest London Mums/Dads" (family bikes)
- "Gravel Riding UK"
- "E-bike London"

**Template post:**
```
🚴 New Bike Shop Open in [AREA]!

Hi everyone, BikeClinique is now open in [LOCATION]!

We offer:
🔧 Expert bike repairs & servicing
🚲 Premium used gravel bikes & e-bikes
📦 Free collection & delivery

First 10 customers get 20% off their first service!

Find us: [link]
Questions? DM me! 👍
```

### 4. Flyers (Design Below)
- Print 500 flyers for £20-30
- Put in:
  - Local cafes
  - Bike racks
  - Community centres
  - Other local shops
  - Estate agents windows

## 🤝 Local Partnerships (Free)

### 1. Cycling Clubs
Message 5 local clubs:
- Offer: "Free basic safety check for club members"
- Ask: "Can we put flyers at your rides?"

### 2. Local Cafes
- Ask: "Can we leave some flyers?"
- Offer: "Bring this flyer for 10% off"

### 3. Other Shops
- Local bike shops (don't compete - they may refer)
- Outdoor shops
- Gyms
- Estate agents

### 4. Community Centres
- Put flyer on notice boards
- Offer free talks: "Bike Maintenance 101"

## 📞 Direct Outreach

### 1. Knocking Doors
- Print 200 leaflets
- Door-drop in your area
- Offer: "£20 off first service"

### 2. Local Businesses
- Email/visit small businesses
- Offer: "Corporate discounts for your team"
- Template:

```
Hi [NAME],

I'm [YOUR NAME] from BikeClinique - local bike shop in [AREA].

We offer priority servicing for local businesses. 
Bring your team's bikes to us - we'll give you 10% off.

Want to chat? Happy to buy you a coffee!

Cheers,
[NAME]
```

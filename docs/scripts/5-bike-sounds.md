# Video Script: "5 Bike Sounds That Mean Trouble"

## HOOK (0-3 seconds)
Option A: "Your bike is TRYING to tell you something"
Option B: "5 sounds your bike makes - and what they mean"
Option C: "Stop riding your bike if you hear this"

## BODY (30-45 seconds)

### Sound 1: Squeaky Brakes (5s)
- Stock: Bike brakes in action
- Text: "SQUEAL = Brake pads need adjusting"

### Sound 2: Clicking Pedals (5s)
- Stock: Pedals/crankset
- Text: "CLICK = Worn bearings"

### Sound 3: Grinding Gears (5s)
- Stock: Bike chain/gears
- Text: "GRIND = Needs lubrication"

### Sound 4: Rattling (5s)
- Stock: Loose bolts/parts
- Text: "RATTLE = Something's loose"

### Sound 5: Whirring (5s)
- Stock: Wheels spinning
- Text: "WHIRR = Wheels need truing"

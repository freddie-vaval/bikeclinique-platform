## Design Specs

### Colors
- **Primary:** #FF6B35 (Orange)
- **Secondary:** #1A1A2E (Dark Navy)
- **Background:** White or #F5F5F5 (Light Gray)
- **Text:** #1A1A2E (Dark Navy)

### Fonts (Canva)
- **Headlines:** Bebas Neue (or similar bold sans-serif)
- **Body:** Open Sans or Roboto

### Size
- A5 (148mm x 210mm) - half A4
- Or A4 (210mm x 297mm)

### Images to Add
1. Bike icon/logo (provided)
2. Photo of your shop (if you have one)
3. Photo of you/mechanic
4. Happy customer with bike (stock photo)

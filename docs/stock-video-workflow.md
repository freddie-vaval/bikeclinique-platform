# BikeClinique Viral Video - Stock Video Workflow

## Where to Get FREE Stock Bike Videos

### 1. CapCut (Easiest)
- Open CapCut → Click **Stock Media** 
- Search: "bike", "cycling", "mechanic", "bicycle"
- Free to use in TikTok/Reels

### 2. Pexels (Free)
- pexels.com/videos/search/bike/
- 100% free, no attribution needed

### 3. Pixabay (Free)  
- pixabay.com/videos/search/bicycle/
- Free for commercial use

### 4. InVideo AI (Paid)
- invideo.io - AI generates videos from text
- Good for faceless content

## Quick Start: Create Your First Video TODAY

### Step 1: Get Stock Footage
Open CapCut → Stock Media → Search "bike repair" or "cycling"

### Step 2: Pick a Script

**Script 1: "5 Bike Sounds That Mean Trouble"**
```
HOOK (0-3s): "Your bike is TRYING to tell you something"
BODY (15-30s): Show 5 stock clips of bikes with text overlays:
- Squeaky brakes = adjustment needed
- Grinding gears = chain needs oil
- Clicking pedals = bearing wear
- Rattling = loose bolts
- Whirring = wheel true needed
CTA: "Follow for more bike tips"
```

**Script 2: "POV: At the Bike Shop"**
```
HOOK (0-3s): "POV: You're at the bike shop and..."
BODY: Stock clips of bike repairs with text overlays
CTA: "Book at bikeclinique.co.uk"
```

**Script 3: "Before & After"**
```
HOOK (0-3s): "This bike went from THIS to THIS"
BODY: Stock dirty bike → clean bike transformation
CTA: "Need your bike done?"
```

## Video Templates (Copy & Paste to CapCut)

### Template A: "Sound Guide"
1. Stock clip: Bike wheels spinning
   - Text: "Squeaky brakes?"
2. Stock clip: Bike chain
   - Text: "Grinding gears?"
3. Stock clip: Bike mechanic working
   - Text: "Book online → bikeclinique.co.uk"

### Template B: "POV/Montage"
1. Stock clip: Customer arriving
2. Stock clip: Mechanic inspecting
3. Stock clip: Repair work
4. Stock clip: Customer happy
   - Text: "That's the vibe"

### Template C: "Transformation"
1. Stock: Dirty bike
   - Text: "BEFORE"
2. Stock: Clean bike
   - Text: "AFTER"
3. Text: "Book your transformation"
